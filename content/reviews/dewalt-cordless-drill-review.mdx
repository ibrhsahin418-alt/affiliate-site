No. This model uses a brushed motor. DEWALT's brushless models (DCD791, DCD777) cost more but offer greater efficiency and longer motor life.

**Can I use other DEWALT batteries?**
Yes. Any DEWALT 20V MAX battery is compatible, including higher-capacity 2Ah, 4Ah, and 5Ah options sold separately.
