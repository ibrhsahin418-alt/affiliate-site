## Buying Advice

**Don't buy based on room size claims alone.** Manufacturers often claim coverage at 2 air changes per hour (ACH). For allergy and asthma sufferers, 4–5 ACH is more effective. This means you may want a purifier rated for twice your actual room size.

**True HEPA matters.** Avoid units labeled "HEPA-type" or "HEPA-like" — they don't meet the 99.97% @ 0.3 micron standard.

**Factor in filter replacement costs.** A cheap purifier with expensive replacement filters can cost more over 3 years than a premium model with cheaper filters.
